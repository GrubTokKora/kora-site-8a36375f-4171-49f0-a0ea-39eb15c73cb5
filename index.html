Our Story

Tradition, crafted with freshness

Rainbow Thai brings together classic Thai flavors and a calm waterfront dining experience in the heart of Westport.

From rich curries and wok specialties to noodle favorites and comforting soups, each dish is prepared for balanced flavor and honest quality.
